Chronology of the Master's Life

August 21, 7 BC (noon)
122:8.1
Jesus is born in Bethlehem.
August 29, 7 BC
122:8.2
Jesus is circumcised and formally named Joshua (Jesus).
March 25, 7 BC
122:2.7
John the Baptist is born in the City of Judah.
June, 8 BC
122:2.2
Gabriel appears to Elizabeth.
October, 4 BC
123:1.2
Jesus returns to Nazareth at about three years and two months old.
April 2, 3 BC
123:1.5
Birth of James, Joseph and Mary's second child.
July-August, 3 BC
123:1.7
Mary flees with Jesus and James to her brother's farm due to an intestinal epidemic in Nazareth.
